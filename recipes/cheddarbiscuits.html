Cheddar Biscuits

Description

Make Red Lobster's delicious cheddar biscuits for yourself using this copycat recipe.

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour
  • A Cup of Shredded Cheddar
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Butter
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Garlic Powder
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. In one bowl, combine the dry ingredients.
  2. In another bowl, combine the wet ingredients.
  3. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and lightly mix.

    4. Note: The mixture is meant to be chunky so be careful not to overmix.
  4. Drop the dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 10 minutes.
  5. Melt the butter and stir in garlic powder and parsley.
  6. Brush the butter topping onto each biscuit.
  7. Bake for another 5 minutes, until the biscuits are golden brown.
