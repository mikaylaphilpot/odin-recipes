Blueberry Muffins

Description

Make yourself some delicious blueberry muffins using this recipe. This recipe will yield 15 muffins.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 of a Cup of Butter
  • 1 Cup of Sugar
  • 3/4 of a Cup of Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 and 3/4 of a Cup of Sifted Flour
  • 2 and a 1/2 Teaspoons of Baking Powder
  • 1/2 a Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1 Cup of Blueberries
  • 1 Tablespoon of Flour

Steps

  1. Beat butter and sugar until creamy.
  2. Add milk and egg and beat until smooth.
  3. Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Add the sifted ingredients to creamed mixture.
  5. Mix creamed mixture until moistened.
  6. Toss blueberries with flour and fold into batter.
  7. Fill a well-greased muffin pan 2/3 full with the batter.
  8. Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes.
