Blueberry Muffins

Description

Make yourself some delicious blueberry muffins using this recipe. This recipe will yield 15 muffins.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 of a Cup of Butter
  • 1 Cup of Sugar
  • 3/4 of a Cup of Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 and 3/4 of a Cup of Sifted Flour
  • 2 and a 1/2 Teaspoons of Baking Powder
  • 1/2 a Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1 Cup of Blueberries
  • 1 Tablespoon of Flour
