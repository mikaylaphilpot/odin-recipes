Chicken Tenders

Description

This is a copycat recipe of Red Lobster's delicious fried chicken tenders.

Ingredients

  • Marinade

    • 6 Medium Garlic Cloves
    • 2 Cups of Buttermilk
    • 1 and a 1/4 Teaspoons of Ground Cumin
    • 1/2 A Teaspoon of Salt
    • 1/2 A Teaspoon of Pepper
    • A 1/4 Teaspoon of Cayenne Pepper
    • 4 to 6 Boneless Chicken Breasts, Cut Into Strips

  • Coating

    • 1 and a 1/2 Cups of Unbleached All-Purpose Flour
    • 1 and 3/4 Teaspoons of Salt
    • 1 and 1/2 Teaspoons of Pepper
    • 1/2 A Teaspoon of Ground Cumin
    • 1 Teaspoon of Cayenne Pepper
    • Safflower Oil (for deep frying)

Steps

  1. Mash garlic with flat side of knife
  2. Place garlic in medium-sized bowl
  3. Add buttermilk, cumin, salt, pepper, and cayenne to bowl
  4. Use whisk to blend ingredients in bowl together
  5. Place chicken strips in 13 by 9-inch glass baking dish
  6. Pour buttermilk mixture over chicken strips and turn the strips to coat it
  7. Cover and chill the chicken strips (in their glass baking dish) at least 8 hours or overnight, turning the strips occasionally
  8. Place rack over baking sheet. Remove chicken from marinade and set on rack. Drain for 10 mintues.
  9. Mix flour, salt, pepper, cumin and cayenne pepper in large bowl.
  10. Toss chicken strips in batches in flour mixture, turning to coat the strips. Shake off excess.
  11. Toss each piece again in flour mixture; shake off excess.
  12. Transfer chicken to rack on baking sheet.
  13. Let stand at least 15 minutes and up to 45 minutes.
  14. Preheat oven to low.
  15. Line baking sheet with paper towels.
  16. Pour oil into heavy large skillet to depth of 3/4 inch.
  17. Heat oil over high heat to 375 degrees F or until small cube of fresh bread sizzles instantly when added.
  18. Add chicken strips to skillet.
  19. Adjust heat so that temperature remains between 340 and 350 degrees F or until small cube of bread sizzles slowly when added.
  20. Fry until chicken is golden brown and cooked through, about 10 minutes.
  21. Using tongs, transfer chicken to prepared sheet and drain. Transfer chicken to platter and serve.
